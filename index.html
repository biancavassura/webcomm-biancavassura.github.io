ABOUT US

Since 1968 our club has been a point of reference for artistic gymnastics in the local area: a place where passion, discipline, and fun come together every day.

Our mission is to support the growth of every athlete in a calm, inclusive, and motivating environment, where everyone can develop their own potential, regardless of their starting level.

Training with us means improving coordination, focus, and self-confidence, learning how to work as part of a team and discovering the beauty of pushing beyond your limits.
