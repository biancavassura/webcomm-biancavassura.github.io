INTRODUCTION

  • I trained for 14 years at A.S.D. Ginnastica Baveno. Now, working here, I saw the need to better showcase its programs and achievements.
  • This inspired the idea of creating a clear and engaging website for athletes, families, and the community.

CONCEPT

Goal

Informative, user-friendly website.

Target audience

  • Parents of young athletes
  • Children and teenagers
  • Adults interested in sports

WEBSITE STRUCTURE

Home | Programs | Our coaches | Our gym | Enrollment

HOME

The homepage is designed to give an immediate overview of our mission and core values, featuring a clean layout with quick access to news and updates.

  • About us
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Engaging video

PROGRAMS

Our programs include courses for all levels. The website provides details of course, age groups, and training schedules.

OUR COACHES

We present our professional staff with their certifications and qualifications, highlighting the passion and experience they bring to every session.

OUR GYM

A dedicated gallery showcases our facilities and high-quality equipment, emphasizing a safe and professional environment for gymnasts of all ages.

ENROLLMENT

This section simplifies the registration process. It contains downloadable forms, medical certificate requirements, and a step-by-step guide for new members.

TECHNOLOGIES USED

The project is built on a solid foundation of standard web technologies.

  • HTML5: semantic structure
  • CSS3: custom styling and animations
  • JavaScript: interactivity and logic
  • Bootstrap 5: responsive grid and components
  • Pandoc: document conversion
  • Reveal.js: HTML presentation framework

HTML5

Used to build the semantic structure of the website, organizing the text and media into logical sections.

Header, nav, main, section, and footer for clear separation of content.

EXTERNAL CSS3

Handles the overall visual identity, such as the color palette, typography, and site-wide layout settings.

Used for custom components, such as hover effects on buttons and specific styles for the navigation menu or the fixed logo.

INTERNAL CSS

Good for single-page specific styles.

JAVASCRIPT

Added to make the website interactive, managing the mobile menu, scroll animations, and form validation.

BOOTSTRAP

A framework used to ensure the website is fully responsive and compatible with all mobile devices and screen sizes.

PANDOC & REVEAL.JS

The combination of Pandoc and Reveal.js allowed me to convert Markdown code into this interactive web presentation.

LIMITATIONS

  • Mainly informative website
  • Limited interactivity
  • Manual content management

CONCLUSIONS

  • This project transforms a personal experience into a digital solution.
  • It improves visibility and communication with athletes, families, and the community.
  • The website provides a solid base for future digital development.
